Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how we collect, use and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us — for example when you contact us through the website. This may include your name, job title, email address and the contents of your message.

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to respond to your enquiries, to provide and improve our services, and — only with your consent — to send you relevant communications.

3. Information sharing

We do not sell, trade or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with service providers who help us operate the website and run our business, provided they agree to keep it confidential.

4. Data security

We apply appropriate measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure or destruction.

5. Your rights

You have the right to access, update or delete your personal information at any time. You may also opt out of receiving communications from us.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on the website. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect how the site functions.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the revised policy on this page.

8. Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please get in touch.
