About

Enhancing your organisation's sustainability

We drive improvements in the Environmental, Social and Governance performance of start-ups and SMEs — ambitious in standard, grounded in your reality.

What we do

A standard set high, a path that fits you

ESG Thread exists to improve the Environmental, Social and Governance performance of start-ups and SMEs. We do that by drawing together international best practice, current research and close work with your key stakeholders — so the approach stays ambitious while still suiting your business and the stage it's at. Our consultant has worked both inside large, listed organisations and within an SME, and brings both vantage points to the table.

Why ESG Thread

Three commitments that shape the work

Affordable

Expert support on your sustainability journey shouldn't be financially prohibitive. Our services are priced to the deliverable — no price floors, no inflated hourly rates.

Capacity-building

What sets us apart from many sustainability consultancies is that we want you to be as independent as possible afterwards. Some work needs ongoing support, but we build internal capacity so ESG practice can grow in-house and your reliance on third parties falls.

Integrated

ESG too often sits in a silo, with the final deliverable detached from its wider context. We'd rather it be seen for what it is when embedded and executed well: a genuine value-add, both financial and non-financial.
